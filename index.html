The Leo Fu Story


Nestled right next to Macleod Trail for almost the past decade, Leo Fu’s prides itself in quitely providing humble authentic food experiences for local, national and international food eaters. We cater all sorts of chinese food experiences ranging from Sichan (Szechuan) flavors to north american chinese food classics.

David Wang as the head chef at Leo Fu’s; has over 15 years of cooking expertise in providing authentic no frills food experiences for a wide range of eaters and looks forward to the future where he will continue his passion on maintaining his simple cooking traditions for all to enjoy.

About

Our restaurant provides curated authentic tastes from Sichuan (Szechuan), China as well as north american style chinese food classics. We pride ourselves in providing humble food for people with all types of tastes. We have been quietly serving local, national and international food eaters at the same spot that was established over 7+ years ago, located right next to Macleod Trail.
